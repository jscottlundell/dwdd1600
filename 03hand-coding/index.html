Outer Darkness Paranormal

Outer Darkness Paranormal began in 2013 as a passionate gathering of amateur ghost hunters who were more interested in the thrill of the unknown than in proving a point. What started as a few friends swapping spooky stories soon morphed into something more substantial. By 2016, the team had expanded and evolved into a well-oiled, highly skilled group of professionals. Our website launch marked the beginning of a new era of serious, respectful paranormal investigation, and since then, we’ve been on a relentless quest to uncover the unexplained.

We pride ourselves on our unique blend of expertise and enthusiasm. Our team consists of three seasoned investigators, each bringing a distinct set of skills to the table. Two of our members are retired military, whose discipline and attention to detail are matched only by their love for the paranormal. They approach each investigation with a combination of precision and courage that ensures we leave no stone—or haunted object—unturned. The third member is our in-house gadget guru, a brilliant inventor whose knack for creating innovative ghost-hunting devices adds a playful edge to our investigations. Whether it's a high-tech spirit box or a custom-built EMF meter, his inventions often lead us to fascinating new discoveries.

We’ve explored countless private homes and businesses across the United States, with a particular focus on central and northern Utah. Every investigation is conducted with the utmost professionalism and respect for our clients and their spaces. Our dedication to unraveling the mysteries of the paranormal is as strong as ever, and we’re always excited for the next opportunity to delve into the unknown and share our findings with those curious about the supernatural.
